BT21 Characters

BT21 is composed of eight characters created by the members of the South Korean boy group BTS. Prince TATA who had been living on planet BT escaped from their royal kingdom as the restrictive lifestyle of their royal family prohibited their creative nature and free spirit. In their mission to spread love all throughout the galaxy, TATA travels to earth with VAN to become a star. In their journey, they meet six idiosyncratic members: RJ, KOYA, SHOOKY, MANG, CHIMMY, and COOKY to form the group 'BT21.' Together, they strive towards their dreams of becoming the most famous stars in the universe while simutaneously spreading love.

TATA

Prince TATA from planet BT has a stretchy body as well as super natural powers. Their goal is to spread love all throughout the universe. Created by BTS V.

RJ

RJ comes from a financially stable family of alpacas due to them selling their fur. RJ is very polite, enjoys wearing parkas, and is happiest when eating. Created by BTS Jin.

KOYA

KOYA is very bright and musically talented. They enjoy sleeping more than anything and is very thoughtful. Created by BTS RM.

SHOOKY

SHOOKY loves pulling pranks on their friends, especially their best friend COOKY. They may be small and look cute, but they are very tough and always willing to defend themselves from anyone that underestimates their strength. Created by BTS Suga.

MANG

MANG expresses the true essence of their character through their dancing. However, veiled behind their mask, their true indentity remains a secret. Rather than showing their true appearance, MANG would prefer to abide to society and wear a mask that they feel would prevent them from being alienated as they were in the past. Created by BTS J-Hope.

CHIMMY

CHIMMY is very innocent and has a passion to always work hard after being abandoned. They overcame this obstacle by strivng hard towards their goals but always making sure to balance that with the right amount of playing. Created by BTS Jimin.

COOKY

Despite COOKY's cute and innocent appearance, they are very rough and strong that many people tend to overlook. They wish to break this streotype by working hard to gain even more strength. Created by BTS Jungkook.

VAN

VAN acts as BT21's protector and vechicle. They help BT21 by safety bringing them to the places in which the members wish to spread love. They appear to know everything about the world due to their wise appearance and is generally very quiet. Created by BTS.
